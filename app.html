When enabled, Live Location lets your trusted contacts know where you are in real time during an emergency. The ProTech system is designed to balance safety and privacy by only sharing your position when an SOS event is triggered or when you explicitly allow it in settings.
Recommended ways to use Live Location:
  1. Before a late-night commute, enable location sharing with at least one trusted contact.
  2. During long trips, keep your jacket charged so updates remain accurate and reliable.
  3. After reaching home safely, you can disable continuous location sharing in Settings.
